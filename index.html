What is our process?

Let's start the conversation to see if we are the right fit for your needs.

Step 1: Contact Us

The first step is for you to fill out the contact form below. We will respond with an email or phone call and together we can determine whether or not to move forward with scheduling a consultation appointment.

Step 2: Complete Forms

After deciding to move forward, you will submit an intake form. After submission, we will email you an invitation to join Therapy Appointment where you will complete the required new patient documentation.

Step 3: Consultation

After completing the new patient documentation, we will contact you to set up a consultation appointment. This appointment is how we determine the best therapy or treatment plan for you. Once a patient, you will use Therapy Appointment to book future appointments.
